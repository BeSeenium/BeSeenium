Detailed Description

This class is the root of the capabilities chain of responsibility, it takes care of defining the chain and, assuming the request fails, passing it to the first handler in the chain. The request then propagates down the chain until it gets handled, or in the worst case scenario, drops off the end of the chain without being handled.
